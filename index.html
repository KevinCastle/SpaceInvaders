This game is one of the all-time classic video games and we can think about it as the first shooter game of all time and obviously the first one to introduced the fighting aliens!

Space invaders was created by Taito, a small Japanese company founded in 1953. One of its first products was a clone of the famous Pong. Toshihiro Nishikado, the father of Space Invaders, was a programmer working on Taito

Nishikado presented an idea about a soldier who, behind some trenches, shoots at enemies that were approaching him. But in Taito they thought maybe it could be too violent and it'll not socially accepted (Just remember was 1978 and the games was quiet) and maybe will carry problems to them. After think about it, he decide change it to aliens and spaceships. Months later, Space Invaders was born.

The mechanics can't be simplest. Behind green cosmic shelds the player have to defeat 5 columns of 11 aliens approaching them faster and faster each time and evading their laser rays, and the player just have 3 lifes to survive. No matters if the mechanics are simple, it result very adictive! The player controls a spaceship in the bottom of the screen and only can move right or left. A third button allow you fire straight to the top, if it impact against an enemy it'll be destroyed. The objective is destroy all of them before they reach you.

Exist three types of enemies:

10 points

20 points

40 points

And a special enemy:

randomly number either 50, 100 or 200 points

The shields that protect the player's spaceship breaks everytime a laser collide its (player laser too!), being sometimes a obstacle and giving a bit of difficulty to the game. At years the players made strategies to survive more time. One of this strategies is deleting invaders columns to columns from one side to the other.

The most important thing about this game is the Hi-Score. The objetive is look for the greatest score since space invaders didn't use time in the game, just 3 lifes to survive.
